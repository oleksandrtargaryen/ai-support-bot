# Appointments policy

Booking is free and takes less than a minute through this bot.

## Cancellation and rescheduling

You can cancel or move your appointment at any time before it starts, free of
charge - just ask the bot. If you are more than 20 minutes late, the slot may
be given away and you will need to rebook.

## What to bring

Please bring your vehicle registration certificate. For warranty claims also
bring the receipt of the original repair.

## While you wait

Most services under one hour can be done while you wait - we have a lounge
with Wi-Fi and coffee. For longer repairs we will message you when the car
is ready.
