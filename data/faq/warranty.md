# Warranty

All repair work comes with a 6-month or 10,000 km warranty, whichever comes
first. Parts purchased through us carry the manufacturer's warranty
(typically 12 months).

The warranty does not cover:

- normal wear of consumables (brake pads, filters, wipers)
- damage caused by accidents or off-road driving
- work done on the car by other workshops after our repair

To make a warranty claim, book a diagnostics appointment through this bot and
bring your receipt.
