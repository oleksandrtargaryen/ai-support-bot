# Payment

We accept cash, credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers
for corporate clients.

Payment is due after the work is completed and you have inspected the car.
For repairs with parts worth over $500 we ask for a 30% prepayment.

## Price estimates

The prices listed for our services cover labor only. Parts and consumables
are billed separately at the current supplier price. You always get a full
estimate and must approve it before we start any work.
